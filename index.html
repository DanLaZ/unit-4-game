Story

The world is being held captive by Dr.Robotnik's evil grasps!

Sonic must collect the last remaining chaos emeralds in order to become Super Sonic and save the world!

Game Rules

End! will show a number that needs to be reached.

Run! shows which number you're currently at.

Click the chaos emeralds to add to your Run! number to match it to the END! number to win.

PAY CLOSE ATTENTION to how much each emerald adds to your Run! number.
